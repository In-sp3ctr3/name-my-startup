## Product Positioning

Namelift is a self-serve SaaS web app for startup name brainstorming. Users enter a short project description and the software generates AI-assisted startup name ideas with automated domain, social, trademark, and web evidence signals.

Namelift is not a consulting service, custom naming agency, brand strategy service, trademark clearance service, legal service, business formation service, marketplace, donation product, or physical-goods business.

## Products And Pricing

### Free Sample

- Price: $0
- Delivery: Automatic inside the Namelift web app
- Includes: 3 AI-assisted startup name ideas once
- Payment required: No
- Card required: No

### Namelift Launch Pack

- Price: $5 one-time
- Delivery: Automatic inside the Namelift web app
- Includes: 50 AI-generated startup name ideas for one startup/project
- Includes evidence signals: domain, social, trademark, and web checks where available
- Billing: No subscription, no recurring charge, no hidden fees
- Services excluded: no consulting, custom naming, legal advice, trademark clearance, business formation, or guaranteed availability

## Fulfillment

Fulfillment is automated and digital. After successful checkout, the customer’s Launch Pack is unlocked/generated inside Namelift. The customer can view the generated names, evidence signals, saved names, and reports in the app.

Live checkout is provider-pending. The product is currently in test/pre-approval mode, and live checkout should only be enabled after payment-provider approval.

## Evidence Disclaimer

Evidence checks are informational signals from available public sources at the time of generation. They may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Namelift does not guarantee that a name is available, protectable, registrable, non-infringing, or safe to use. Before adopting a name, customers should do their own due diligence and consult a qualified trademark attorney if needed.

Avoid these claims:

- trademark-safe
- legally cleared
- guaranteed available
- guaranteed non-infringing
- we name your company
- custom naming service
- brand consulting
- legal review

## Refund Summary

Because Launch Packs are generated digital content, purchases are generally final once delivered. Refund requests are reviewed within 7 days for non-delivery, duplicate charges, or technical issues that prevent access to the purchased Launch Pack. The merchant of record/payment provider may also issue refunds or handle chargebacks according to its own policies.
